Our Background

Cleanliness is Our Talent

Being incorporated in 2004 in partnership with an Italy-based corporation, Biokem′s mission is to ensure quality cleaning-related services be provided to different hotel departments across hygiene and sanitation. Out of our deeply-rooted understanding of the environment sustainability-related benefits of natural substances, which cannot be found in bio-chemical substances, Biokem prides itself in being a pioneering enterprise in harnessing natural substances in cleaning and sanitation. In addition, this method ensures a proper process be in place to minimize all types of industrial waste, which tend to harm the surrounding environment. Furthermore, we provide complete assurance of quality and safety due to protective mechanisms followed by a worker using both materials and equipment.

In order to maintain individuals’ safety, equipment efficiency and preservation of surrounding environment at a lower cost, Biokem produces various products that serve all departments in terms of practical‚ variable and specialized solutions for all cleaning-related issues that tend to encounter our clients who aim to ensure seamless cleaning and sanitation process in place using state-of-the-art technologies and safe methods. Lastly, in view of our slogan “Cleanliness is Our Talent”, we are extremely confident that our suite of products would satisfy our clients and even exceed their expectations.
